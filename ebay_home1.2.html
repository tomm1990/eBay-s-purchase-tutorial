    How to open a new PayPal account



    PayPal inc. is a company which operates a online payment method within a simple platform that connects both front and end users. This company suggest to transfer any amount of money from one account to another.
    This method comes to replace the traditional payment methods and makes transactions easier to handle.

    Secure? YES.

    PayPal signifies any user to login with a personal username and password, but this company validates an additional key which provided only to the logging in specific user.
    Open your internet browser and enter www.PayPal.com
    Click on the "Sign up for free" link.
    In the following window you have to choose one option :
    • Personal Account
    • Business Account


    Please choose the Business Account.
    This option allows you to send and receive payments.
    Click "Continue" to proceed.
    In the next step you will have to provide the following elements as follows.
    If any of the follows is already taken, you should provide another values.
    Please choose your business type.
    Recommended is "Sole Proprietorship"
    Please provide your business name.
    Valid values: at least one alphabet letter or number and no more than 7 digits.
    Please provide the date your business was registered in the registrar of companies.
    You may fill in whatever you like.
    Please provide your City
    At the category section, Please choose "Services – other".
    At the Subcategory section, Please choose "Services".
    Those options allows you to sell almost anything.
    Please provide the date your business was registered in the registrar of companies.
    You may fill in whatever you like.
    Please provide your first and last names.
    Please enter you date of birth ( required by law )
    Please provide your telephone number
    At the Home address section, Please choose the first option.
    Finally, Click on "Continue"
    Please provide your email address
    (if you don't have any – you can create a new one)
    Please provide a valid password and re-enter it.
    In this section, you will have to choose two questions that the answer is known to you only.
    Please prove that you are not a robot by filling up the characters in the picture as follows.
    Please view and read the User Agreement and Privacy Policy.
    Fill in the empty box to agree, and press "Agree and Continue".
    After this installation you will receive an email which you need to approve in order to proceed this.
    At the end of it, your Paypal account would be implemented in your ebay account.
