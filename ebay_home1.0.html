eBay's Manual

    Required Elements:


    In order to start buying, please make sure that you have the following elements:

    1. Internet connection :


    First and most important elements is the internet connection. Please verify that you have a strong available Internet connection. If you don't – contact your Internet supplier.


    2. Email account :


    Secondly, you must have an e-mail address. This allows ebay's to send you notifications, bills, privacy agreements and more. If you don't have any of those, you can easily open a new one.


    3. Electronic method of payment (preferred):


    Usually, buyers pay via online pocket ( such as PayPal ) and Credit cards. Some sellers offers the option to pay cash when they meet up, but this is very rare.
    You can easily open a new electronic payment method.
    Note that each of the methods is secure. Which means, your money is in safe hands.


    4. Motivation to search-find-buy the most cool stuff you have ever wanted with a small amount of money and effort.
