    How to create a email address



    In case you don't have any email address, this chapter will teach you how to open an new account in one of the email accounts suppliers : Gmail, Hotmail, Walla!, Yahoo! MAIL and more.

    This example shows how to open a new account in gmail.com


    Open your internet browser and enter www.gmail.com
    Click on the "Create Account" link.
    In the next step you will have to provide the following elements as follows.
    If any of the follows is already taken, you should provide another values.
    Full name: First and Last.
    Choose a email account nickname.
    Create and confirm your personal password
    Provide your birthday and gender
    Prove that you are not a robot by inserting the numbers within the pic
    Agree to the Google Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and click "Next step"
    Congratulations! You have a new email address. With this, you can proceed to the next steps.
