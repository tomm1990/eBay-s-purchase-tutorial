eBay's Manual

    This will guide you to create a new ebay account



    Open your internet browser and enter to www.ebay.com.

    At the left side, click 'register'.

    In the following window you will have to fill in your first and last names, Email address (if you don't have any – "please get one), Create a valid password and re-enter it once again.
    We strongly advice you to read and agree the eBay User Agreement and User Privacy Notice and that you are at least 18 years old.
    Make sure you have filled in the check mark
    To continue please click on Register button down below.

    That’s it! You created a new account in ebay!
